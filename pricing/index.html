Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge implementation fees?

Implementation is included in the initial first-month billing for Scaling and Enterprise tiers. Pilot tier requires self-serve API integration.

How does the per-transaction fee work?

The 0.15% fee applies to the gross volume orchestrated through our API. It replaces traditional payment gateway risk fees, consolidating your costs.

Can we upgrade mid-contract?

Absolutely. You can move from Pilot to Scaling instantly via the dashboard. Enterprise tier transitions require a scoping call.
